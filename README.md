## How To Use

When you first open the Application you will be greeted with a gui

<img width="656" alt="instructions_gui" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/70408199/141923630-8228b266-c8cd-4383-a06e-f14f6b282e9b.png">

The price of KatanaKoins is shown at the top under the title

***Price is constanly updating***

<img width="790" alt="coin_price" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/70408199/141926377-e3e4f857-23e7-4595-92d2-d33d29b57739.png">


***MAKE SURE YOU READ THE INSTRUCTIONS BEFORE STARTING***

If not the instructions will be in the bottom right of the Application

<img width="656" alt="instructions_gui" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/70408199/141926335-ccefe10b-1039-4374-9d99-c481345ac485.png">

The App should Look Like This:

<img width="1379" alt="fullscreen" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/70408199/141924080-5f77d966-1baa-44d0-a522-7485cef92985.png">

This is the create wallet section where you will get you private key and wallet address.
This section will also give you access to recent transactions and you wallet balance.

<img width="664" alt="create_wallet" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/70408199/141924336-396b575d-dd06-4b15-bdea-293451981597.png">

To get your ***PRIVATE KEY*** click on the ***CREATE WALLET*** button

After that your private key will be created

<img width="646" alt="private_key_gui" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/70408199/141924706-2ae31d1c-7385-45a3-bafb-a186b44eeedc.png">

***MAKE SURE YOU COPY AND STORE YOUR PRIVATE KEY IN A SECURE PLACE
YOU WILL NOT HAVE ACCESS TO YOUR PRIVATE KEY AFTER THIS***

Next you will paste your private key into the login section

<img width="361" alt="login_private_key" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/70408199/141924995-13c5177e-6f5a-490b-9ad7-f1a65fbf47e5.png">

Then click ***CREATE WALLET***

<img width="647" alt="copy_public_key" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/70408199/141925054-1a092cdb-9340-48ef-86c2-35260181ef18.png">

Copy and paste your public adress into the Login and you will be granted access to more features

<img width="357" alt="login_both_filled" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/70408199/141925199-ffe08647-9047-4964-9914-4f27a2edce8c.png">

You will now be able to see recent transactions from your address, Your wallet balance and you will given the ability to buy and send coins

To see balance just click on ***SHOW WALLET***

<img width="653" alt="wallet_info_green" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/70408199/141926116-d9297f75-1f26-415d-a4fd-e144e7f8beac.png">


To buy coins all you need is to be logged in and enter an amount to buy

<img width="187" alt="buy_coins_btn" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/70408199/141925512-d368c78f-93ac-4fb3-8b8c-e155ad305902.png">

<img width="626" alt="buy_coins" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/70408199/141925523-615f5fb9-5a79-48c6-9fc0-0c4cd8e3684d.png">

***RECENT TRANSACTIONS***

<img width="629" alt="transactions1" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/70408199/141925549-fa6b2138-7f5b-422a-ae21-7c43ff666714.png">

To send coins you need to be logged in, enter an amount, and have the public address of the person recieving the coins

<img width="195" alt="send_coins_btn" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/70408199/141925740-adf49e01-9df8-4945-baf5-7d7f586969a4.png">

<img width="626" alt="buy_coins" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/70408199/141925819-b98e3bfa-19de-462f-afc1-228455cee9a3.png">
